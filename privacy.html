Privacy Policy

JN Traveler respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information collected through this website is used and protected.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form on this website, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and travel preferences.

How Your Information Is Used

Information you provide may be used to respond to inquiries, plan travel services, send requested digital guides, or provide occasional updates related to travel inspiration and services offered by JN Traveler.

Email Communications

If you opt in to receive emails, you may occasionally receive updates, travel inspiration, or new digital guide announcements. You may unsubscribe from these communications at any time.

Information Protection

JN Traveler takes reasonable measures to protect personal information and does not sell or share personal data with third parties except where necessary to provide services requested by the user.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes to the website or applicable regulations.
